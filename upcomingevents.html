August - Monthly Ride to the Rock Store
Hello to everyone that is in the Motorcycle Community. I want to invite you on a easy ride up to The Rock Store then end at Neptune's Net for CMC's Monthly Ride of September. This ride is open to Beginners to Advance riding experiences. Come ride with us to enjoy some awesome twisties, stop at a few locations to take photos and enjoy some awesome food at famous riding destinations, we may bump into Jay Leno! Invite your friends and family. This should be a nice day with great weather to ride and to make new friends! All motorcycles are welcome to join us!!
Anyone is welcome to join, such as small Bikes like 250's, dual sport, super-moto, sport-bike, and cruisers.
This will be an easy ride up to Malibu to head to the Rock Store through Mulholland Hwy to Neptunes Net to end off the Ride. We will pull off on some turnout to take photo's at different spots on the mountain and get some fresh air and hangout.
Mileage to Destinations:
  • Jack in the Box to Mobile Gas -- 21.9 Miles
  • Mobil Gas to Chevron Gas -- 13.1
  • Chevron Gas to Rock Store -- 29.4
Total Miles: 81.7

  • We will pull in together for gas before we go onto the freeway and before we head up the mountain.
  • Wear Pants or Riding Gear.
  • Bring something to hydrate with or snack for the road.
  • Will go over road rules and hand signs Before we start the ride.
