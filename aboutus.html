About California Motorcycle Community (C.M.C.)

Hello All CMC Members,
It is awesome to share the road with such a collective group of Motorcycle enthusiasts. We are so fortunate to be in California and have the great riding weather that we do. It’s always exciting to meet new members and share stories of how they are got into motorcycling, it really is addicting in many ways. Trying to create an environment for people to be social and connect is at times a struggle as we have different points of view and opinions about motorcycle riding or just life in general. It’s always been a goal for the California Motorcycle Community to create a space to share the love of motorcycle riding, to bring like minded people together and that will always be a core value for the community. In keeping with that mission it’s best to discuss some of the good and bads about moderating a large community.
It’s essential that we are free to express all things Motorcycle related to the community, opinions, suggestions, and constructive criticism is all welcome. It’s healthy to welcome different points of view, sometimes you just need to throw it out there, as there may be someone struggling just like you in the moment. Although this freedom can at times create the opposite effect and invites negativity. This is a good time to list the types of subjects that are just not beneficial or productive to the community.
  • No hate speech, bashing or bullying
  • No Politics, keep it Motorcycle related
  • No Nudity or Inappropriate content
Any of the listed above will be removed from the group feed. If there is something that has to be said that you feel goes against those three subjects you are more than free to post it on your personal FB profile. I encourage you to report this type negative content to the admins and we’ll address it, I rather not anyone be lured into responding to such, report it before things escalate.
Now for the good, keep it moto and keep doing what you’re doing. I see posts about meets, rides, spontaneous get together, suggestions and positive vibes. There’s nothing I enjoy more than to be bored at home or idle and to see someone post up “Lets Ride”. Motorcycle riding just brings out the best in me and I’m sure it’s the same for you all as well.
Thank you all again so much for all that you do and all that you’re planning to do. It’s our honor to help you all in any way possible and we look forward to seeing you and riding with you soon.
California Motorcycle Community is a safe, welcoming community for any biker, rider, or motorcyclists on the road in California. Contact us to join!
